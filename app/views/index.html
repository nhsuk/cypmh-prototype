Mental health support for children and young people

If you’re a child or young person and think you might need help for how you are feeling (your mental health) there are NHS-funded services that can help.

There are also lots of charities and organisations that you can go to for support.

If you’re a parent or carer, you can read more here about the help available to children and young people, and how they can access it. You can also find out what support is available to you.
