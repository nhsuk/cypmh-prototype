Getting help on the NHS

Children and young people’s mental health services (CYPMHS) are NHS services available for people under 18 who need professional help to manage how they are feeling. You might also hear them called children and adolescent mental health services (CAMHS). They usually offer a range of support including talking therapies such as counselling, family therapy and medicines.

Read more about NHS services

Other types of help and support

Mental health charities and support organisations provide a wide range of services – from helplines and counselling to advocacy, information and self-help resources.
